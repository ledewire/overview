Architecture Decision Records

ADRs document the significant technical choices made in Ledewire — what was decided, why, and what alternatives were considered. They create an institutional memory that helps the team understand context over time.

How to add a new ADR: Copy the template below, save it as docs/adr/NNN-short-title.md, and add a link to the index on this page. ADRs are immutable once accepted — to change a decision, create a new ADR that supersedes the old one.

ADR Template

Use this template when writing a new decision record. Keep each ADR focused on a single decision. 

# ADR-NNN: Short Title of Decision

**Status**: Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by ADR-NNN
**Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
**Deciders**: [names of key people involved]

## Context
What forces are at play? What problem are we solving?
What constraints exist (technical, organizational, time)?

## Decision
What are we doing?

## Consequences

**Positive:**
- What becomes easier or better?

**Negative:**
- What becomes harder or worse?
- What tradeoffs are we accepting?

**Neutral:**
- What changes but is neither better nor worse?

## Alternatives Considered

- Pros: ...
- Cons: ...

**Option 2: [Name]**
- Pros: ...
- Cons: ...
